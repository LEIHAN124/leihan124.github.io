abstract: >-
  We present CoStage, an AI-supported embodied co-creation platform that combines LLM-based story generation with multi-robot stage performance to support children's storytelling and spatial imagination. By enabling children to direct robot actors on a tangible 360° stage, CoStage transforms written stories into embodied performances. A within-subjects study (N = 24) showed that robot enactment increased immersion, enhanced directorial agency, supported spatial understanding, and encouraged iterative storytelling. Our findings highlight the potential of embodied AI co-creation for fostering children's narrative engagement and spatial cognition.

abstract: >-
  This research explores how AI-assisted interactive systems can support architectural heritage understanding and preservation. Focusing on the UNESCO World Heritage site Kaiping Diaolou and Villages, we conducted a formative study (N=14) to identify challenges and opportunities for AI in heritage engagement. Based on these insights, we designed Gen-Diaolou, an AI-assisted system for heritage learning and preservation awareness. Findings from a pilot study (N=18) and a museum field study (N=26) suggest that Gen-Diaolou can enhance visitors' understanding of heritage evolution and promote preservation awareness, offering design implications for future human–AI collaboration in digital cultural heritage.

abstract: >-
  This work explores the use of VR art and interactive storytelling to create an immersive narrative experience based on a poetry collection. Through a VR game, the project examines the relationship between labor and social networks in the context of technological innovation. By combining textual expression, visual effects, and empathetic storytelling, the experience connects virtual and physical worlds while demonstrating the potential of poetry-driven VR narratives.
